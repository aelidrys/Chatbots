Restaurant description

Legend Restaurant is a cozy and modern dining spot located in essaouira, 
offering a warm ambiance and exceptional culinary experiences. With a seating capacity 
of 80 guests and a floor space of approximately 200 square meters, the restaurant 
specializes in a blend of Mediterranean and continental cuisine. It also features 
a carefully curated selection of beverages including fresh juices, teas, and specialty 
coffees. Whether you're looking for a relaxed lunch, a family dinner, or a casual drink 
with friends, Legend Restaurant is your perfect destination.
